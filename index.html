The Institutions of Learning

When ever we go from one organization to another there is an interface. from one school to another school because the family moved. from High School to University or trade school. From University or trade school to company. All of these have had one organization tell the other organization where the individual in question is in their understanding and skills. Because of the complex nature of this undertaking and the number of individuals involved these have had to rely on simple standards that can be easily understood and compared. This has proven to have drawbacks but we accepted it at first because it was the best we could do, then because it would be to dificult to change, than because it had so much time behind it that no one questioned it.

In this day and age we can do much more than could be done before. And also we are frustrated with the limitations of the current system.
